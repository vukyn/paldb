# Sphere Assembly Line II

> An upgraded sphere line — tight management greatly raises sphere output over
> the first [[sphere-assembly-line|Sphere Assembly Line]].

The high-tier sphere station. It doubles the power draw (400 electricity/sec)
for much higher sphere throughput. Drains a little sanity (SAN −0.15); defense 4.

## Notable crafts

All capture spheres, from the [[pal-sphere|Pal Sphere]] upward.

## Build

Unlocked at **Technology Lv 35**. Build workload: 120000 ([[handiwork|Handiwork]]).
Requires an electricity supply to operate.
