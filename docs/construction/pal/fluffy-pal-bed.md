# Fluffy Pal Bed

> A soft mid-game Pal bed that Pals love to sleep on.

A fluffy bed that Pals can get a good rest on. Pals can rest on it when injured,
or to sleep at night. Pals love sleeping on fluffy beds like this. Upgrade from
the [Straw Pal Bed](straw-pal-bed.md).

## Rest

Restores **2.5 HP** per tick and recovers **0.1** [Sanity](../../mechanics/sanity.md)
(SAN Rate). Defense 3.

## Build

Unlocked at **Technology Lv 24**. Build workload: 10000 ([[handiwork|Handiwork]]).
