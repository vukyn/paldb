# Hot Spring

> The dedicated base relaxation facility — Pals soak here to recover Sanity.

A facility where Pals on a base can rest. It restores SAN after a long day of
hard work. The most efficient passive [Sanity](../../mechanics/sanity.md)
recovery available in the early-mid game — build one to stop your workers
drifting toward Depressed. A [[high-quality-hot-spring|High Quality Hot Spring]]
upgrade offers an even higher rate later.

## Use

Place at a base; idle and off-duty Pals automatically soak in it to recover
**Sanity** at a rate of **0.5**. Pairs with a stocked [[feed-box|Feed Box]] and
a [[straw-pal-bed|Pal bed]] per worker to keep base morale high. Defense 2.

## Build

Unlocked at **Technology Lv 9**. Build workload: 8000 ([[handiwork|Handiwork]]).
