# Large Pal Bed

> A big fluffy bed roomy enough for even large Pals to sleep soundly.

A fluffy bed that Pals can get a good rest on. Pals can rest on it when injured,
or to sleep at night. Even large Pals can sleep soundly on a bed like this. A
late-game upgrade from the [Fluffy Pal Bed](fluffy-pal-bed.md); matched in size
by the [Ancient Pal Bed](ancient-pal-bed.md).

## Rest

Restores **5 HP** per tick and recovers **0.15** [Sanity](../../mechanics/sanity.md)
(SAN Rate). Its large footprint lets big Pals rest on it, unlike the smaller
beds. Defense 3.

## Build

Unlocked at **Technology Lv 36**. Build workload: 50000 ([[handiwork|Handiwork]]).
