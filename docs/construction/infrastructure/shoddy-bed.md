# Shoddy Bed

> An early human bed — sleep through the night to skip to morning.

A shoddy bed for humans. Use it when injured or to sleep at night. You won't be
able to sleep soundly without a roof over your head. The starter human bed,
later replaced by the [Fine Bed](fine-bed.md).

## Use

Lie down to sleep through the night and recover. Requires a roof overhead for a
sound sleep. Defense 2.

## Build

Unlocked at **Technology Lv 3**. Build workload: 2000 ([[handiwork|Handiwork]]).
