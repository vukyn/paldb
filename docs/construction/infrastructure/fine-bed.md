# Fine Bed

> An upgraded human bed — a fluffier night's sleep than the Shoddy Bed.

A fluffy bed for humans. Use it when injured or to sleep at night. You won't be
able to sleep soundly without a roof over your head. The upgrade from the
[Shoddy Bed](shoddy-bed.md).

## Use

Lie down to sleep through the night and recover. Requires a roof overhead for a
sound sleep. Defense 3.

## Build

Unlocked at **Technology Lv 28**. Build workload: 20000 ([[handiwork|Handiwork]]).
