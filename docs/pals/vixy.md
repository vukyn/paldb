# Vixy <small>#6</small>

> The idol of Palpagos Island. If you bully a Vixy, you best be prepared to
> become enemies with the whole world.

An early [[elements|Neutral]] Pal — Paldeck #6, size XS, rarity 2. Its high
Support (140) and its dig-up partner skill make it a farm-value Pal rather than
a fighter.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

farm.

## Combat

[[elements|Neutral]] element — weak to Dark, strong against nothing. Uniformly
low combat stats (Health/Attack/Defense 70); not a fighter. Its standout stat is
Support 140. At level 80 it reaches Health 3700–4540, Attack 520–646, Defense
470–596.
