# Swee <small>#35</small>

> Crawling along the ground, it eats microscopic organic matter and later
> discharges whatever has no nutrition. Use it as a mop for cleaning and everyone
> wins!

A tiny [[elements|Ice]] Pal — Paldeck #35, size XS, rarity 1. Mostly a support
piece: its partner skill powers up [[sweepa|Sweepa]], and it breeds the Ice
variant [[hangyu-cryst|Hangyu Cryst]].

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Fluffy** — while Swee is in your party, it raises the **Attack and Defense of
[[sweepa|Sweepa]]**.
