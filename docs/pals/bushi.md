# Bushi <small>#85</small>

> Its body becomes a blade upon death, to be taken up by the next generation. If
> someone other than a Bushi wields this blade, the soul within torments them
> until they are driven mad.

A [[elements|Fire]] samurai Pal — Paldeck #85, size M, rarity 7. A strong melee
attacker and a solid [[lumbering|Lumbering]] worker. Breeds with
[[sootseer|Sootseer]] into the Fire/Dark [[bushi-noct|Bushi Noct]].

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="5" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **5 / 10** — a fairly heavy eater (Food 380).

## Partner skill

**Brandish Blade** — when activated, it strikes the targeted enemy with a
quick-draw **Iaigiri** slash carrying a **×1.1–2.5** damage multiplier. The
multiplier scales with skill level:

| Lv | Iaigiri × |
|:--:|:---------:|
| 1 | 1.1 |
| 2 | 1.3 |
| 3 | 1.6 |
| 4 | 2.0 |
| 5 | 2.5 |

hitter: Attack 125, MeleeAttack 100, but only 80 Health/Defense. At level 80 it
reaches Health 4100–5060, Attack 850–1075, Defense 530–674.
