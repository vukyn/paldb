# Wixen Noct <small>#78B</small>

> Due to its upbringing, it no longer cares about whether its food is cooked enough.
> This Pal just prefers its food as sweet as possible. Not on good terms with
> [[katress-ignis|Katress Ignis]].

The [[elements|Fire]]/[[elements|Dark]] variant of [[wixen|Wixen]] (#78) —
Paldeck #78B, size M, rarity 6. A glass-cannon mage whose partner skill turns the
player into a Dark attacker.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="4" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **4 / 10** — moderate appetite.

## Partner skill

**Black Fox Lord** — when activated, **Wixen Noct changes the player's attack type
to [[elements|Dark]]** and increases the player's Attack by **30~50%** (scales with
level):

| 5 | +50% | Dark |

## Combat

[[elements|Fire]]/[[elements|Dark]] dual type — weak to Water and Dragon. A glass
cannon: very high Attack 110 and Support 120 but weak Melee 50 / Defense 85. At
level 80 it reaches Health 4500–5580, Attack 760–958, Defense 560–713.
