# Beegarde <small>#67</small>

> A servant that pledges loyalty to [[elizabee|Elizabee]]. Any that harm the queen
> are expelled from the hive at once, and it will gladly give its life to protect
> her.

A [[elements|Grass]] Pal — Paldeck #67, size M, rarity 4. The worker bee of the
hive: it powers up [[elizabee|Elizabee]] in your party and farms Honey at a base.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="3" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **3 / 10** — modest appetite (base Food 230).

## Partner skill

**Worker Bee** — while in your party it **raises [[elizabee|Elizabee]]'s Attack**
(pairs with Elizabee's own Queen Bee Command). While assigned to a [[ranch|Ranch]]
it periodically drops **Honey** — the quantity scales with the Ranch's level, from
×1 at Lv 1 up to ×3–10 at Lv 10 (always 100%).
