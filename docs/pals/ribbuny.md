# Ribbuny <small>#44</small>

> A Pal that's never without a bright smile. Occasionally, its tentacles get tied
> up in knots by [[cattiva|Cattiva]]'s pranks. During those moments, its
> expression turns demonic.

A [[elements|Neutral]] Pal — Paldeck #44, size XS, rarity 1. A support Pal that
buffs Neutral teammates and speeds up base Handiwork. Its Grass variant is
[[ribbuny-botan|Ribbuny Botan]] (#44B).

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Happy-Go-Lucky Bunny** — while in your party it raises the **Attack of Neutral
Pals by 15–30%**; while assigned at a base it raises the [[handiwork|Handiwork]]
Work Suitability level of **all other base Pals by +1** (neither effect stacks).
The Handiwork bonus is a flat **+1**; the Attack bonus scales with level:

| Lv | Neutral Attack + |
|:--:|:----------------:|
| 1 | 15% |
| 2 | 17% |
| 3 | 20% |
| 4 | 24% |
| 5 | 30% |

## Combat

[[elements|Neutral]] element — weak to Dark, strong against nothing. Solid melee
(MeleeAttack 100) but low Attack/Defense (65/70). At level 80 it reaches Health
3900–4800, Attack 490–607, Defense 470–596.
