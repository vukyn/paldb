# Jolthog <small>#15</small>

> It releases the electricity stored in its body when under attack. The voltage
> it generates exceeds 10 million volts, making it more lethal than any
> conventional heavy firearm when thrown.

An early [[elements|Electric]] Pal — Paldeck #15, size XS, rarity 1. A throwable
grenade of a Pal and an early source of power generation.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Jolt Bomb** — when activated, Jolthog equips to the player as a throwable; hurl
it at an enemy for an [[elements|Electric]] **explosion on impact**. Explosion
value scales with skill level, with an Awakening damage bonus from Lv 2:

| Lv | Value | Awakening |
|:--:|:-----:|-----------|
| 1 | 100 | — |
| 2 | 110 | Damage Up: S |
| 3 | 130 | Damage Up: M |
| 4 | 160 | Damage Up: L |
| 5 | 200 | Damage Up: XL |

## Combat

[[elements|Electric]] element — strong against Water, weak to Ground. Low
all-round stats (Health/Defense 70, Attack 75); its damage comes from the Jolt
Bomb throw, not melee. At level 80 it reaches Health 3700–4540, Attack 550–685,
Defense 470–596.
