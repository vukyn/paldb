# Foxcicle <small>#95</small>

> On nights when the aurora is visible, it looks up towards the sky and begins to
> howl a beautiful song. This does, however, leave it vulnerable to attacks from
> enemies.

An [[elements|Ice]] Pal — Paldeck #95, size S, rarity 5. A base worker with strong
Cooling, a Ranch Ice-Organ producer, and an Attack buff for Ice teams.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="4" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **4 / 10** — moderate appetite.

## Partner skill

**Aurora Guide** — while Foxcicle is in your party, the Attack of all
[[elements|Ice]] Pals is boosted by **15~30%** (scales with level, **does not
stack**). It also **sometimes drops [[ice-organ|Ice Organ]] when assigned to a
Ranch**:

| Lv | Ice Attack + |
|:--:|:------------:|
| 1 | +15% |
| 2 | +17% |
| 3 | +20% |
| 4 | +24% |
| 5 | +30% |

## Combat

[[elements|Ice]] type — weak to Fire. Tanky for its size (Defense 105) with Melee
100 / Attack 95. At level 80 it reaches Health 4500–5580, Attack 670–841, Defense
680–869.
