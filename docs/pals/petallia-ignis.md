# Petallia Ignis <small>#89B</small>

> A Pal that burns away into a pile of ash at the end of its life. The surrounding
> soil turns incredibly fertile, letting vegetation thrive for the next decade.

The [[elements|Grass]]/[[elements|Fire]] variant of [[petallia|Petallia]] —
Paldeck #89B, size M, rarity 9. A premium base worker and party defender: it heals
the player and shields the party from Grass damage.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="5" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **5 / 10** — eats a fair amount (base Food 450).

## Partner skill

**Passion of the Flower Spirit** — on activation, soothing blossoms restore the
player's **Health by 80–90%**. While in your party, the player and party Pals take
**15–30% less [[elements|Grass]] damage** and are **immune to Ivy-Covered** (does
not stack). Both scale with level:

| Lv | Grass dmg reduction | Player heal |
|:--:|:-------------------:|:-----------:|
| 1 | 15% | 80% |
| 2 | 17% | 82% |
| 3 | 20% | 84% |
| 4 | 24% | 86% |
| 5 | 30% | 90% |

## Combat

Dual [[elements|Grass]]/[[elements|Fire]] — takes extra damage from Water and
Fire, hits hard with Attack 105. At level 80 it reaches Health 4700–5840, Attack
730–919, Defense 650–830.

## Breeding

CombiRank 820. Hatches from a **[[huge-verdant-egg|Huge Verdant Egg]]**. A unique
combo — bred from **[[petallia|Petallia]] + [[bushi|Bushi]]**. See [[breeding]].
