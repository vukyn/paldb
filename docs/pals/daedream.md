# Daedream <small>#22</small>

> It puts those it is interested in to sleep and shows them an endless stream of
> happy dreams. Those who fall under its spell are never to wake until death takes them.

An early [[elements|Dark]] Pal — Paldeck #22, size XS, rarity 1. A cheap all-round
base worker whose partner skill provides a free, capture-safe attacking familiar.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Dream Chaser** — while Daedream is in your party, it **appears near the player**
and attacks hostile enemies with [[elements|Dark]] magic bullets. Its damage
multiplier scales with level, and crucially the bullets **will not reduce a
target's Health below 1** — so it can soften wild Pals for capture without killing
them:

| Lv | Attack multiplier | Awakening |
|:--:|:-----------------:|-----------|
| 1 | ×1.5 | — |
| 2 | ×1.6 | Damage Up: S |
| 3 | ×1.8 | Damage Up: M |
| 4 | ×2.1 | Damage Up: L |
| 5 | ×2.5 | Damage Up: XL |

## Combat

[[elements|Dark]] type — weak to Dragon. Low stats (Attack 75, Defense 60) but
high Melee 100; its real value is the capture-safe Dream Chaser familiar. At level
80 it reaches Health 3700–4540, Attack 550–685, Defense 410–518.
