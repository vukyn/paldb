# Fuack <small>#5</small>

> Using its own body water, this Pal can create waves anywhere. It body surfs
> when in a hurry, but the resulting speed often ends in a fatal collision.

An early [[elements|Water]] Pal — Paldeck #5, size XS, rarity 1.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Surfing Slam** — when activated, Fuack body surfs towards an enemy and slams
into them. The slam multiplier scales with skill level, with an Awakening damage
bonus from Lv 2:

| Lv | Value | Awakening |
|:--:|:-----:|-----------|
| 1 | 1.5 | — |
| 2 | 1.6 | Damage Up: S |
| 3 | 1.8 | Damage Up: M |
| 4 | 2.1 | Damage Up: L |
| 5 | 2.5 | Damage Up: XL |

## Combat

attack (MeleeAttack/Attack 80) with lower Health and Defense (60). At level 80 it
reaches Health 3300–4020, Attack 580–724, Defense 410–518.
