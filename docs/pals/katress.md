# Katress <small>#79</small>

> A witch-like Dark cat whose black magic squeezes extra loot out of Neutral Pals
> and can save your Pal Spheres.

A [[elements|Dark]] Pal — Paldeck #79, size M, rarity 6. A strong Handiwork /
Medicine Production base worker with a farming-and-capture partner skill.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="4" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **4 / 10** — moderate appetite.

## Partner skill

**Mystical Black Magic** — while Katress is in your party, [[elements|Neutral]]
Pals drop **40~80% more items** when defeated, and thrown [[pal-sphere|Pal Spheres]]
have a **10~50% chance not to be consumed**. Both scale with level and **do not
stack**:

| Lv | Neutral drop + | Sphere save |
|:--:|:--------------:|:-----------:|
| 1 | +40% | 10% |
| 2 | +50% | 15% |
| 3 | +60% | 20% |
| 4 | +70% | 30% |
| 5 | +80% | 50% |
