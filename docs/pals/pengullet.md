# Pengullet <small>#17</small>

> The feathers of this Pal have all but disappeared, but sadly, its desire to fly
> has remained as strong as ever. Even now, it tries to fly again in any way it
> can.

An early [[elements|Water]]/[[elements|Ice]] Pal — Paldeck #17, size XS, rarity 1.
Its Water/Electric variant is [[pengullet-lux|Pengullet Lux]] (#17B).
Its partner skill turns it into rocket-launcher ammunition.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Pengullet Launcher** — when activated, the player equips a **Rocket Launcher**
that fires Pengullet as ammunition; it **explodes on impact** and is then
incapacitated. Unlocked at **Technology Lv 17**. Explosion value scales with
level, with an Awakening damage bonus from Lv 2:

| Lv | Value | Awakening |
|:--:|:-----:|-----------|
| 1 | 250 | — |
| 2 | 285 | Damage Up: S |
| 3 | 325 | Damage Up: M |
| 4 | 400 | Damage Up: L |
| 5 | 500 | Damage Up: XL |

## Combat

[[elements|Water]]/[[elements|Ice]] dual type — weak to Electric (Water) and Fire
(Ice). Low all-round stats (Health/Defense 70, Attack 75); its burst comes from
the launcher partner skill. At level 80 it reaches Health 3700–4540, Attack
550–685, Defense 470–596.
