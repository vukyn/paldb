# Teafant <small>#11</small>

> Large amounts of water pour from what is thought to be its nose, though some
> say that it is, in fact, just snot. This has sparked a fierce debate among Pal
> scholars.

An early [[elements|Water]] Pal — Paldeck #11, size M, rarity 1. A support Pal
whose partner skill auto-heals you in a pinch.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Soothing Shower** — while in your party, when the player's **Health drops below
30%** Teafant restores **20–40% of Max Health** (does not stack; 120-second
cooldown after each activation). Heal amount by level:

| Lv | Heal (Max HP) |
|:--:|:-------------:|
| 1 | 20% |
| 2 | 24% |
| 3 | 28% |
| 4 | 32% |
| 5 | 40% |

## Combat

[[elements|Water]] element — strong against Fire, weak to Electric. Low all-round
stats (Health/Defense 70, Attack 60); not a fighter. At level 80 it reaches
Health 3700–4540, Attack 460–568, Defense 470–596.
