# Pengullet Lux <small>#17B</small>

> Its feathers have all but vanished and it can no longer fly. Its longing for the
> sky curdled into jealousy — so it harnessed electricity to strike down those who
> still soar.

The [[elements|Water]]/[[elements|Electric]] variant of [[pengullet|Pengullet]] —
Paldeck #17B, size XS, rarity 2. Like Pengullet it doubles as living rocket-launcher
ammo, but its Electric typing also makes it a base power source. Per its lore it
became a loyal follower of [[penking-lux|Penking Lux]].

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Pengullet Lux Launcher** — on activation the player **equips Pengullet Lux to
fire from a Rocket Launcher**; it explodes on impact and is knocked out afterward.
The blast damage scales with level:

| Lv | Rocket damage | Awakening |
|:--:|:-------------:|-----------|
| 1 | 300 | — |
| 2 | 335 | Damage Up: S |
| 3 | 375 | Damage Up: M |
| 4 | 450 | Damage Up: L |
| 5 | 550 | Damage Up: XL |

## Combat

Dual [[elements|Water]]/[[elements|Electric]] — weak to Grass and Ground. Modest
stats (Attack 80). At level 80 it reaches Health 3700–4540, Attack 580–724, Defense
470–596.
