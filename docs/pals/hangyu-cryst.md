# Hangyu Cryst <small>#38B</small>

> Its gigantic arms can tear through blocks of ice. Legend says great sinners were
> once tied up in the town square and had their hair torn out by a Hangyu Cryst as
> brutal public humiliation.

The [[elements|Ice]] variant of [[hangyu|Hangyu]] — Paldeck #38B, size XS, rarity
2. Like its Ground original, its value is the glider-upgrading partner skill.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Winter Trapeze** — while in your party it modifies the equipped glider and
enables **slow ascension while gliding** (the Ice version of Hangyu's Flying
Trapeze). Higher levels lower the glider's gravity scale and improve stamina:

| Lv | Gravity scale | Glider SP |
|:--:|:-------------:|:---------:|
| 1 | — | — |
| 2 | 0.009 | 9 |
| 3 | 0.008 | 8 |
| 4 | 0.007 | 7 |
| 5 | 0.006 | 6 |

reaches Health 4100–5060, Attack 580–724, Defense 470–596.
