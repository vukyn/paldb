# Penking Lux <small>#18B</small>

> Its body changed color after gaining electricity to improve underwater hunting.
> As a result, it lost the affection of [[pengullet|Pengullet]] — but strangely
> enough, it now has a loyal follower: [[pengullet-lux|Pengullet Lux]].

The Water/Electric variant of [[penking|Penking]] (#18) — Paldeck #18B, size M,
rarity 7. An electrified upgrade with maxed 100 combat stats across the board and
a Water weak-point damage partner skill.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="4" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **4 / 10** — moderate appetite.

## Partner skill

**Unyielding Storm Commander** — while Penking Lux is in your party, the player's
and all Pals' [[elements|Water]] damage to enemy **weak points** is boosted by
**25~40%**. Bonus scales with the skill's level and **does not stack** with
another Penking Lux:

| Lv | Weak-point Water dmg |
|:--:|:--------------------:|
| 1 | +25% |
| 2 | +27% |
| 3 | +30% |
| 4 | +34% |
| 5 | +40% |

Strong pick for a Water-focused party hunting weak-point-heavy targets.

## Combat

[[elements|Water]]/[[elements|Electric]] dual type. Maxed base stats (Attack /
Defense / Health 100) with Work Speed and Support both 100. At level 80 it reaches
Health 4900–6100, Attack 700–880, Defense 650–830.
