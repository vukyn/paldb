# Chikipi <small>#3</small>

> Extremely weak and far too delicious. Alongside [[lamball|Lamball]] it is one
> of the weakest Pals — and no matter how many are hunted, they just keep
> appearing.

An early [[elements|Neutral]] Pal — Paldeck #3, size XS, rarity 1. Its value is
food: it lays [[egg|Egg]]s on a [[ranch|Ranch]] and drops [[chikipi-poultry|Chikipi Poultry]].

## Combat

Neutral element, so it is [[elements|weak to Dark]] and strong against nothing.
Base stats are very low (Health 60, Attack/Defense 60). At level 80 it reaches
Health 3300–4020, Attack 460–568, Defense 410–518 — among the weakest fighters
in the game.
