# Sootseer <small>#135</small>

> It was once believed that as the world approached its end, the flame would
> weaken. Doomsayers rejoiced every time the flame dimmed — but it always turned
> out to be a prank by Sootseer. At least, for now.

A [[elements|Dark]]/[[elements|Fire]] Pal — Paldeck #135, size M, rarity 7. A
strong all-round base worker and the source of a party-wide Neutral-damage cut.
Breeds with [[bushi|Bushi]] into [[bushi-noct|Bushi Noct]].
