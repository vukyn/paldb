# Sweepa <small>#36</small>

> While hibernating, a great number of [[swee|Swee]] hide within its voluminous
> body hair — the most ever recorded is 101.

A large [[elements|Ice]] Pal — Paldeck #36, size L, rarity 6. A rideable mount
whose Attack and Defense scale with the number of [[swee|Swee]] in your party, and
a solid Cooling worker at a base.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="4" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **4 / 10** — a large Pal, eats a moderate amount.

## Partner skill

**King of Fluff** — Sweepa **can be ridden**, and its **Attack and Defense rise by
12–24% for every [[swee|Swee]]** in your party (pairs with Swee's own Fluffy buff).
The per-Swee bonus scales with level:

| Lv | Attack & Defense + per Swee |
|:--:|:---------------------------:|
| 1 | 12% |
| 2 | 13% |
| 3 | 15% |
| 4 | 19% |
| 5 | 24% |

## Combat

[[elements|Ice]] element — weak to Fire. Even stats (Attack/Defense 90) that scale
hard when partied with [[swee|Swee]]. At level 80 it reaches Health 4900–6100,
Attack 640–802, Defense 590–752.
