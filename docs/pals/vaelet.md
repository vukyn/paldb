# Vaelet <small>#66</small>

> The castle was filled with the king's favorite flowers. A great battle ensued
> and flames neared the castle; amidst the chaos, the spirit of a flower
> appeared. — from the fairy tale *The King's Flower*.

A rare [[elements|Grass]] Pal — Paldeck #66, size M, rarity 8. A strong all-round
base worker (six work suitabilities) whose partner skill boosts Ground-Pal loot
and turns a Ranch into a seed farm.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="5" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **5 / 10** — eats a fair amount (base Food 380).

## Partner skill

**Purification of Gaia** — while in your party, [[elements|Ground]] Pals drop
**40–80% more items** when defeated (does not stack). While assigned to a
[[ranch|Ranch]] it periodically drops **various crop seeds**. The defeat-drop
bonus scales with level:

| Lv | Ground drop + |
|:--:|:-------------:|
| 1 | 40% |
| 2 | 50% |
| 3 | 60% |
| 4 | 70% |
| 5 | 80% |

## Ranch seed production

At a [[ranch|Ranch]] it drops one of the following seeds each cycle (chances are
fixed; the **quantity** scales with the Ranch's level, from ×1–2 at Lv 1 up to
×4–11 at Lv 10):

| Seed | Chance |
|------|:------:|

## Combat

(120) for its tier. At level 80 it reaches Health 4900–6100, Attack 700–880,
Defense 770–986.
