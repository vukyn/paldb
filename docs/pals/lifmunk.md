# Lifmunk <small>#4</small>

> Intelligent as a five-to-seven-year-old child, which makes it a wonderful
> partner — though more than a few have turned a weapon on their owner once they
> learned to use one.

An early [[elements|Grass]] Pal — Paldeck #4, size XS, rarity 1. A jack-of-all-
trades base worker (five suitabilities) that also fights alongside you via its
gun partner skill.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="3" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **3 / 10** — low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Lifmunk Recoil** — when activated, Lifmunk leaps onto the player's head and
fires a submachine gun that follows up your attacks. Unlocks a submachine gun
schematic at **Technology Lv 11**. The follow-up damage scales with level:

| Lv | Main value | Awakening |
|:--:|:----------:|-----------|
| 1 | 15 | — |
| 2 | 16 | Damage Up: S |
| 3 | 18 | Damage Up: M |
| 4 | 21 | Damage Up: L |
| 5 | 25 | Damage Up: XL |

## Combat

Grass element — [[elements|strong against Water/Ground, weak to Fire/Ice]]. Base
stats are uniformly low (Health 75, Attack/Defense 70). At level 80 it reaches
Health 3900–4800, Attack 520–646, Defense 470–596 — a support Pal, not a
front-line fighter.
