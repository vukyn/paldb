# Croajiro <small>#9</small>

> Those who disgrace the honor of the herd are forced to take their own life. By
> piercing their inflated vocal sac with a tree branch, they are blown away into
> the atmosphere and return to the afterlife.

A [[elements|Water]] Pal — Paldeck #9, size XS, rarity 4. A launch-pad partner
that doubles as a burst-damage enabler.
