# Elizabee <small>#68</small>

> A chosen queen who rules over [[beegarde|Beegarde]]s. A never-ending stream of
> servants will work themselves to death for the pleasure of serving their queen.

A rare [[elements|Grass]] Pal — Paldeck #68, size L, rarity 8. An excellent
all-round base worker whose partner skill scales its own Attack with each
[[beegarde|Beegarde]] in the party.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="6" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **6 / 10** — a large Pal that eats a lot (base Food 340).

## Partner skill

**Queen Bee Command** — this Pal's **Attack rises by 12–24% for every
[[beegarde|Beegarde]]** in your party. The per-Beegarde bonus scales with level:

| Lv | Attack + per Beegarde |
|:--:|:---------------------:|
| 1 | 12% |
| 2 | 13% |
| 3 | 15% |
| 4 | 19% |
| 5 | 24% |
