# Melpaca <small>#20</small>

> Don't be fooled by this Pal's fluffy appearance. A hypersonic kick from one of
> its long legs may send you flying to the other side of the world.

A [[elements|Neutral]] Pal — Paldeck #20, size M, rarity 3. A rideable mount and
Ranch wool producer whose partner skill supports [[kingpaca|Kingpaca]].

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="2" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **2 / 10** — low appetite.

## Partner skill

**Pacapaca Wool** — a rideable mount. While in your party, it increases
[[kingpaca|Kingpaca]]'s **Defense and Movement Speed**. It also **sometimes drops
[[wool|Wool]] when assigned to a Ranch**, with the yield scaling up by level (×2
at Lv 1 up to ×4–11 at Lv 10, always 100%). Unlocked at **Technology Lv 7**.
