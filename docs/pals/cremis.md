# Cremis <small>#8</small>

> Compared to Lamball, it has finer wool and a temperament more suited for
> domestication. However, it has historically always been kept as a pet.
> Cuteness is considered a virtue.

An early [[elements|Neutral]] Pal — Paldeck #8, size XS, rarity 1. A finer-wooled
cousin of [[lamball|Lamball]] with a party-wide Neutral Attack buff.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

15–30%** (does not stack with another Cremis). It also **sometimes drops Wool**
when assigned to a [[ranch|Ranch]]. Attack bonus by level:
