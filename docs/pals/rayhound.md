# Rayhound <small>#98</small>

> At full speed, it could be mistaken for a bolt of lightning. When two Rayhounds
> collide, a thunderous roar echoes through the air.

An [[elements|Electric]] Pal — Paldeck #98, size M, rarity 5. A fast rideable
mount whose partner skill rewards a full Electric party with extra speed.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="4" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **4 / 10** — moderate appetite.

## Partner skill

**Lightning Shepherd** — a rideable mount that can **double-jump** while mounted.
Its Movement Speed rises by **5~25%** for **each other [[elements|Electric]] Pal**
in your party (Rayhound itself excluded). Unlocked at **Technology Lv 30**. Bonus
per Electric party member scales with level:

| Lv | Move Speed / Electric Pal |
|:--:|:-------------------------:|
| 1 | +5% |
| 2 | +9% |
| 3 | +13% |
| 4 | +18% |
| 5 | +25% |

Best paired with an all-Electric team for maximum travel speed.
