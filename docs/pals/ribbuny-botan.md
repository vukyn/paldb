# Ribbuny Botan <small>#44B</small>

> Always wears a cheerful smile. Sometimes, thanks to [[cattiva|Cattiva]]'s
> pranks, they end up tangled in tentacles and have to fight their way out — but
> even in tough spots they keep smiling.

The [[elements|Grass]] variant of [[ribbuny|Ribbuny]] — Paldeck #44B, size XS,
rarity 1. A base worker built for planting and crafting, with a partner skill
that boosts Grass weak-point damage and weapon-crafting speed.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Ground 'n' Pound** — while in your party it raises the **Grass damage** the
player and their Pals deal to enemy weak points by **25–40%** (does not stack).
While working at a **Weapon Workbench, Weapon Assembly Line, or related
facility** it boosts crafting efficiency by **200–400%**. Both scale with level:

| Lv | Grass weak-point dmg + | Craft speed + |
|:--:|:----------------------:|:-------------:|
| 1 | 25% | 200% |
| 2 | 27% | 220% |
| 3 | 30% | 260% |
| 4 | 34% | 320% |
| 5 | 40% | 400% |

## Combat

[[elements|Grass]] element — weak to Fire, strong against Ground. Solid melee
(MeleeAttack 100) but low Attack/Defense (65/70). At level 80 it reaches Health
4100–5060, Attack 490–607, Defense 470–596.
