# Kingpaca Cryst <small>#21B</small>

> With a heart of ice, this Pal is terrible at expressing its emotions. A solitary
> individual is pitiable — seen as too clumsy in the eyes of a [[melpaca|Melpaca]].

The [[elements|Ice]] variant of [[kingpaca|Kingpaca]] (#21) — Paldeck #21B, size XL,
rarity 9. A tanky rideable mount and strong Cooling worker.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="4" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **4 / 10** — moderate appetite.

## Partner skill

**Coldhearted King** — a rideable mount whose own **Defense and Movement Speed**
rise by **5~14%** for **each [[melpaca|Melpaca]] in your party** (scales with
level):

| Lv | Defense & Move Speed / Melpaca |
|:--:|:------------------------------:|
| 1 | +5% |
| 2 | +7% |
| 3 | +9% |
| 4 | +11% |
| 5 | +14% |

## Passive skills

- **Heavyweight** — Defense +20% and immune to knockback.

## Combat

[[elements|Ice]] type — weak to Fire. Tanky (Health 120, Melee 100) with the
Heavyweight passive (+20% Defense, knockback immunity). At level 80 it reaches
Health 5700–7140, Attack 610–763, Defense 590–752.

- [[kingpaca|Kingpaca]] + [[reindrix|Reindrix]] → **Kingpaca Cryst**

See [[breeding]].
