# Hoocrates <small>#19</small>

> Often lost in thought, it sometimes finds it difficult to sleep. *"I think,
> therefore I am."*

An early [[elements|Dark]] Pal — Paldeck #19, size XS, rarity 1. A cheap Gathering
worker whose partner skill buffs a Dark team's Attack.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Dark Knowledge** — while Hoocrates is in your party, the Attack of all
[[elements|Dark]] Pals is boosted by **15~30%**. Bonus scales with level and
**does not stack**:

| Lv | Dark Attack + |
|:--:|:-------------:|
| 1 | +15% |
| 2 | +17% |
| 3 | +20% |
| 4 | +24% |
| 5 | +30% |
