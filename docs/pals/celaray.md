# Celaray <small>#7</small>

> Riding the wind, this Pal travels where it pleases. Should it find a partner
> along the way, the encounter will mark its journey's end.

A mid-game [[elements|Water]] Pal — Paldeck #7, size M, rarity 3. Prized less for
combat than for its glider-boosting partner skill.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="2" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **2 / 10**.

## Partner skill

**Zephyr Glider** — while Celaray is in your party it modifies the equipped
glider: it **prevents all fall damage** and extends the duration of high-speed
gliding. The Awakening bonus raises glider performance from Lv 2:

| Lv | Fall damage | Awakening |
|:--:|-------------|-----------|
| 1 | Fully prevented | — |
| 2 | Fully prevented | Glider Performance Boost: S |
| 3 | Fully prevented | Glider Performance Boost: M |
| 4 | Fully prevented | Glider Performance Boost: L |
| 5 | Fully prevented | Glider Performance Boost: XL |

Glider tuning by skill level (higher speed, lower gravity, slower stamina drain):

| Lv | Max Speed | Gravity Scale | Glide SP drain |
|:--:|:---------:|:-------------:|:--------------:|
| 1 | 600 | 0.016 | 10 |
| 2 | 750 | 0.014 | 8.5 |
| 3 | 900 | 0.012 | 7 |
| 4 | 1050 | 0.01 | 5.5 |
| 5 | 1200 | 0.008 | 4 |
