# Croajiro Noct <small>#9B</small>

> The Croajiro who have forsaken their honor gather and form splinter groups.
> They will use any underhanded means to take down their chosen prey. If you
> encounter a pack of Croajiro Noct in the dead of the night, it's your life
> that's on the line.

A [[elements|Water]]/[[elements|Dark]] dual-element variant of
[[croajiro|Croajiro]] — Paldeck #9B, size XS, rarity 5. Keeps the launch-pad trick
but trades the raw attack buff for a Dark weak-point damage boost. A **unique
breeding** result, not caught in the wild.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="2" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **2 / 10**.

## Partner skill

**Shadow Stance** — when activated, Croajiro Noct puffs up its abdomen to store
energy; climbing on top propels the player high into the air. While it is in your
party it also **boosts the player's and Pals' Dark damage against enemy weak
points by 25–40%** (does not stack). Effect by level:

| Lv | Dark weak-point dmg + | Value |
|:--:|:---------------------:|:-----:|
| 1 | 25% | 2500 |
| 2 | 27% | 3000 |
| 3 | 30% | 3500 |
| 4 | 34% | 4500 |
| 5 | 40% | 5500 |

## Combat

[[elements|Water]]/[[elements|Dark]] dual type — weak to Electric (Water) and
Dragon (Dark). Strong offense (Attack 110, MeleeAttack 100) with solid Defense 90.
At level 80 it reaches Health 4300–5320, Attack 760–958, Defense 590–752.

## Breeding

CombiRank 1860. Hatches from a **Large Damp Egg**. Produced by the unique combo
[[croajiro|Croajiro]] + [[bushi-noct|Bushi Noct]]. See [[breeding]].
