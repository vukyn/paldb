# Petallia <small>#89</small>

> A Pal that turns into a massive plant at the end of its life. Once every ten
> years a beautiful flower blooms and a new Petallia is born.

A rare [[elements|Grass]] Pal — Paldeck #89, size M, rarity 8. Its Grass/Fire
variant is [[petallia-ignis|Petallia Ignis]] (#89B). A premier base
worker: it heals the player on activation and lifts every base Pal's Planting.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="5" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **5 / 10** — eats a fair amount (base Food 450).

## Partner skill

**Blessing of the Flower Spirit** — on activation, soothing blossoms restore the
player's **Health by 75–85%**. While assigned at a base it raises the
[[planting|Planting]] Work Suitability level of **all other base Pals by +1**
(does not stack). The Planting bonus is a flat **+1**; the heal scales with level:

| Lv | Player heal |
|:--:|:-----------:|
| 1 | 75% |
| 2 | 77% |
| 3 | 79% |
| 4 | 82% |
| 5 | 85% |

stats (Defense 100). At level 80 it reaches Health 4900–6100, Attack 670–841,
