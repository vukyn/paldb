# Jolthog Cryst <small>#15B</small>

> It releases the cold air stored in its body when under attack. The frigid blast
> it radiates is cold enough to freeze the surrounding air and easily pierces
> through any attacker's body.

An [[elements|Ice]] variant of [[jolthog|Jolthog]] — Paldeck #15B, size XS, rarity
2. A throwable Ice bomb and an early source of Cooling. A **unique breeding**
result, not caught in the wild.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Cold Bomb** — when activated, Jolthog Cryst equips to the player as a throwable;
hurl it for an [[elements|Ice]] **explosion on impact**. Explosion value scales
with skill level, with an Awakening damage bonus from Lv 2:

| Lv | Value | Awakening |
|:--:|:-----:|-----------|
| 1 | 100 | — |
| 2 | 110 | Damage Up: S |
| 3 | 130 | Damage Up: M |
| 4 | 160 | Damage Up: L |
| 5 | 200 | Damage Up: XL |

## Combat

[[elements|Ice]] element — strong against Dragon, weak to Fire. Low all-round
stats (Health 70, Attack 75, Defense 80); its damage comes from the Cold Bomb
throw. At level 80 it reaches Health 3700–4540, Attack 550–685, Defense 530–674.

## Breeding

CombiRank 2850. Hatches from a **Frozen Egg**. Produced by the unique combo
[[jolthog|Jolthog]] + [[pengullet|Pengullet]]. See [[breeding]].
