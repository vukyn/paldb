# Wixen <small>#78</small>

> With the power of light, it produces arcane phenomena. It prefers to eat food
> well-done and is particularly hostile towards [[katress|Katress]].

A [[elements|Fire]] Pal — Paldeck #78, size M, rarity 6. A glass-cannon Fire mage
whose partner skill turns the player into a Fire attacker.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="4" data-max="10"></span>

## Combat

[[elements|Fire]] type — weak to Water. A glass cannon: very high Attack 110 and
Support 120 but weak Melee 50 / Defense 80. At level 80 it reaches Health 4500–5580,
Attack 760–958, Defense 530–674.
