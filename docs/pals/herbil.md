# Herbil <small>#10</small>

> When in doubt, punch it till it works — that's Herbil's approach to fixing
> things. With strength perfectly tuned for humans, sometimes just one punch is
> enough to restart a stopped heart.

A [[elements|Grass]]/[[elements|Neutral]] Pal — Paldeck #10, size XS, rarity 3.
A support Pal whose partner skill can revive you in a pinch.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Herbil Pulse** — while in your party, if the player is **incapacitated** Herbil
heals them back up, restoring **30–60% of Max Health** — an automatic self-revive
on a cooldown. Higher levels heal more and shorten the cooldown:

| Lv | Heal (Max HP) | Cooldown | Awakening |
|:--:|:-------------:|:--------:|-----------|
| 1 | 30% | 700 | — |
| 2 | 36% | 660 | Cooldown Reduction: S |
| 3 | 42% | 620 | Cooldown Reduction: M |
| 4 | 48% | 580 | Cooldown Reduction: L |
| 5 | 60% | 520 | Cooldown Reduction: XL |

## Combat

[[elements|Grass]]/[[elements|Neutral]] dual type — weak to Fire (Grass) and Dark
(Neutral). Balanced melee (MeleeAttack 100) with modest Attack/Defense (75). At
level 80 it reaches Health 4100–5060, Attack 550–685, Defense 500–635.
