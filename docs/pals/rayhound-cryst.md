# Rayhound Cryst <small>#98B</small>

> Sprinting across the tundra at full speed kicks up a blinding blizzard, leading
> to constant [[rayhound-cryst|Rayhound Cryst]] collisions due to zero visibility.

The [[elements|Ice]] variant of [[rayhound|Rayhound]] (#98) — Paldeck #98B, size M,
rarity 6. A fast rideable mount that buffs an Ice party's Defense.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="4" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **4 / 10** — moderate appetite.

## Partner skill

**Snow Shepherd** — a rideable mount that can **double-jump** while mounted. While
in your party, the Defense of all [[elements|Ice]] Pals is raised by **15~30%**.
Bonus scales with level and **does not stack**; from Lv 2 an Awakening also grants
a ride-speed bonus. Unlocked at **Technology Lv 32**:

| Lv | Ice Defense + | Ride Speed (Awakening) |
|:--:|:-------------:|:----------------------:|
| 1 | +15% | — |
| 2 | +17% | +10% |
| 3 | +20% | +12% |
| 4 | +24% | +15% |
| 5 | +30% | +20% |

## Combat

[[elements|Ice]] type — weak to Fire. High offense (Attack 105, Melee 100) with
softer Defense 80. At level 80 it reaches Health 4500–5580, Attack 730–919,
Defense 530–674.

## Breeding

CombiRank 1600. Hatches from a **[[large-frozen-egg|Large Frozen Egg]]**. Unique combo:

- [[rayhound|Rayhound]] + [[foxcicle|Foxcicle]] → **Rayhound Cryst**

See [[breeding]].
