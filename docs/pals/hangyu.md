# Hangyu <small>#38</small>

> Its gigantic arms can rip apart even iron. Legend says serious criminals were
> once strung up in a public square for a Hangyu to tear the skin from their
> bones.

A [[elements|Ground]] Pal — Paldeck #38, size XS, rarity 1. Its Ice variant is
[[hangyu-cryst|Hangyu Cryst]] (#38B). Its real value is the partner skill: it
upgrades your glider and lets you gain altitude mid-glide.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Flying Trapeze** — while Hangyu is in your party it modifies the equipped
glider and enables **slow ascension while gliding**. Higher levels lower the
glider's gravity scale (drift down slower / climb) and improve stamina drain:

| 4 | — | 0.007 | 7 |
| 5 | — | 0.006 | 6 |
