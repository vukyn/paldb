# Bushi Noct <small>#85B</small>

> Its body becomes a blade upon death, to be taken up by the next generation. If
> the Bushi who inherits it has a weak heart, the voices of the dead will drive
> it mad. The madness turns it into Bushi Noct that endlessly wanders.

A [[elements|Fire]]/[[elements|Dark]] dual-element variant of [[bushi|Bushi]] —
Paldeck #85B, size M, rarity 7. A hard-hitting melee attacker and top-tier
[[lumbering|Lumbering]] worker. A **unique breeding** result, not caught in the
wild.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="5" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **5 / 10** — a fairly heavy eater (Food 380).

## Partner skill

**Void Blade** — when activated, it strikes the targeted enemy with a quick-draw
**Iaigiri** slash carrying a **×1.1–2.5** damage multiplier. While it is in your
party it also raises the player's **melee weapon damage by 30–65%**, but only
while **out of combat** (does not stack). Both values scale with level:

| Lv | Melee weapon + (out of combat) | Iaigiri × |
|:--:|:------------------------------:|:---------:|
| 1 | 30% | 1.1 |
| 2 | 35% | 1.3 |
| 3 | 42% | 1.6 |
| 4 | 51% | 2.0 |
| 5 | 65% | 2.5 |

## Combat

[[elements|Fire]]/[[elements|Dark]] dual type — weak to Water (Fire) and Dragon
(Dark). A glass-cannon attacker: high Attack (130) and MeleeAttack (100) but only
80 Health/Defense. At level 80 it reaches Health 4100–5060, Attack 880–1114,
Defense 530–674.

## Breeding

CombiRank 840. Hatches from a **Large Scorching Egg**. Produced by the unique
combo [[bushi|Bushi]] + [[sootseer|Sootseer]]. It is itself a breeding parent of
[[croajiro-noct|Croajiro Noct]]. See [[breeding]].
