> A walk up a hill tends to end with this Pal tumbling back down. This causes it
> to become dizzy and unable to move, making it easy to capture and kill. As a
> result, this Pal has tumbled down to the very bottom of the food chain itself.

## Combat

Neutral element, so it is [[elements|weak to Dark]] and strong against nothing.
Base stats are uniformly low (Health/Attack/Defense 70). At level 80 it reaches
Health 3700–4540, Attack 520–646, Defense 470–596 — a bottom-tier fighter.
