# Penking <small>#18</small>

> Surprisingly, it is unrelated to [[pengullet|Pengullet]]. However, since this
> Pal gets pampered as it pleases, for now it's doing its best to strut arrogantly.

A mid-tier [[elements|Water]]/[[elements|Ice]] Pal — Paldeck #18, size M, rarity 6.
Despite the penguin looks it is **not** an evolution of [[pengullet|Pengullet]].
A capable all-round base worker with a strong farming partner skill against Fire Pals.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="4" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **4 / 10** — moderate appetite.

## Partner skill

**Brave Sailor** — while Penking is in your party, [[elements|Fire]] Pals drop
**40~80% more items** when defeated. Bonus scales with the skill's level and
**does not stack** with another Penking:

| Lv | Drop bonus |
|:--:|:----------:|
| 1 | 40% |
| 2 | 50% |
| 3 | 60% |
| 4 | 70% |
| 5 | 80% |

Handy for farming materials off Fire Pals — pair it with a strong Water attacker.

## Combat

[[elements|Water]]/[[elements|Ice]] dual type — weak to Electric (Water) and Fire
(Ice). Well-rounded stats (Attack/Defense/Health 95) with Work Speed and Support
both 100. At level 80 it reaches Health 4700–5840, Attack 670–841, Defense 620–791.
