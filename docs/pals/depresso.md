# Depresso <small>#16</small>

> It has few friends because of the perpetually grouchy look on its face, but it
> is in fact kindhearted. Some have seen it feeding [[vixy|Vixy]] who have strayed
> from their pack.

An early [[elements|Dark]] Pal — Paldeck #16, size XS, rarity 1. A jack-of-many
base worker that can super-charge its own work speed.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

[[ranch|Ranch]]. Self work-speed bonus by level:
