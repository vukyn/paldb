# Gumoss <small>#12</small>

> A strange Pal with a body resembling tree sap. It gradually loses strength if
> it has nothing to cover its body with, eventually rotting away.

An early [[elements|Grass]]/[[elements|Ground]] Pal — Paldeck #12, size XS,
rarity 1. Its partner skill is a big quality-of-life boost for logging runs.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Logging Assistance** — while in your party it boosts **logging efficiency by
30–50%** and cuts the **weight of all wood types by 40–60%** (does not stack).
Great to carry on tree-farming trips. Values by level:

| Lv | Logging + | Wood weight − |
|:--:|:---------:|:-------------:|
| 1 | 30% | 40% |
| 2 | 35% | 45% |
| 3 | 40% | 50% |
| 4 | 45% | 55% |
| 5 | 50% | 60% |

## Combat

[[elements|Grass]]/[[elements|Ground]] dual type — weak to Fire (Grass) and Grass
(Ground). Decent melee (MeleeAttack 100) but low Attack/Defense (70). At level 80
it reaches Health 3700–4540, Attack 520–646, Defense 470–596.
