# Kingpaca <small>#21</small>

> [[melpaca|Melpaca]] serve this Pal. Contests between Kingpaca offer up their
> vassals as a wager — those seen alone are the losers of such contests.

The "king" of the alpaca line — a large [[elements|Neutral]] Pal, Paldeck #21,
size XL, rarity 8. A tanky rideable mount that grows stronger with [[melpaca|Melpaca]]
in the party.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="4" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **4 / 10** — moderate appetite.

## Partner skill

**King of Muscles** — a rideable mount whose own **Defense and Movement Speed**
rise by **5~14%** for **each [[melpaca|Melpaca]] in your party** (scales with
level):

| Lv | Defense & Move Speed / Melpaca |
|:--:|:------------------------------:|
| 1 | +5% |
| 2 | +7% |
| 3 | +9% |
| 4 | +11% |
| 5 | +14% |

## Passive skills

- **Heavyweight** — Defense +20% and immune to knockback.

## Combat

[[elements|Neutral]] type — weak to Dark. Tanky (Health 120, Melee 100) with the
Heavyweight passive (+20% Defense, knockback immunity). At level 80 it reaches
Health 5700–7140, Attack 610–763, Defense 590–752.
