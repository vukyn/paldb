# Fuack Ignis <small>#5B</small>

> With a belly built for friction, this Pal sparks flames as it body surfs. But
> when it gets carried away, it often ends up a rolling fireball.

A [[elements|Water]]/[[elements|Fire]] dual-element variant of [[fuack|Fuack]] —
Paldeck #5B, size XS, rarity 2. A **unique breeding** result, not caught in the
wild.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Fire Tackle** — when activated, Fuack Ignis fire surfs towards an enemy and
slams into them. The slam multiplier scales with skill level, with an Awakening
damage bonus from Lv 2:

| Lv | Value | Awakening |
|:--:|:-----:|-----------|
| 1 | 1.0 | — |
| 2 | 1.1 | Damage Up: S |
| 3 | 1.3 | Damage Up: M |
| 4 | 1.6 | Damage Up: L |
| 5 | 2.0 | Damage Up: XL |

watering, plus Handiwork and Transporting for general chores.

## Combat

[[elements|Water]]/[[elements|Fire]] dual type — weak to Electric (Water) and
Water (Fire). Strong melee (MeleeAttack 100, Attack 85) with lower Health and
Defense (60). At level 80 it reaches Health 3300–4020, Attack 610–763, Defense
410–518.

## Breeding

CombiRank 2300. Hatches from a **Damp Egg**. Produced by the unique combo
[[fuack|Fuack]] + [[flambelle|Flambelle]]. See [[breeding]].
