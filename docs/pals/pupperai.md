# Pupperai <small>#13</small>

> Pupperai territorial disputes begin with a staredown and end in a single, quick
> strike. Training them to "stay" is risky business, as the "go" command triggers
> that same attack instinct.

An early [[elements|Ground]] Pal — Paldeck #13, size XS, rarity 2. A companion
Pal whose partner skill sharpens your melee damage.
