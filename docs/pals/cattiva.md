# Cattiva <small>#2</small>

> At a glance it looks full of confidence, but it is in fact weak and cowardly.
> Being toyed with by a Cattiva is, in many ways, the greatest of disgraces.

An early [[elements|Neutral]] Pal — Paldeck #2, size XS, rarity 1. Its real use
is the extra carry weight its partner skill grants.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Cat Helper** — while Cattiva is in your party, it helps carry supplies,
raising your max carrying capacity by 100–200. Does not stack (only one Cattiva
counts). The bonus scales with the skill level:

| Lv | Max carry + |
|:--:|:-----------:|
| 1 | 100 |
| 2 | 120 |
| 3 | 140 |
| 4 | 160 |
| 5 | 200 |

## Combat

Neutral element, so it is [[elements|weak to Dark]] and strong against nothing.
Base stats are uniformly low (Health/Attack/Defense 70). At level 80 it reaches
Health 3700–4540, Attack 520–646, Defense 470–596 — a bottom-tier fighter.
