# Clovee <small>#14</small>

> Some individuals once had three or more leaves on their foreheads. Regarded as
> symbols of good fortune, those with more leaves were heavily hunted, leading
> the species to evolve fewer leaves over time.

An early [[elements|Grass]]/[[elements|Neutral]] Pal — Paldeck #14, size XS,
rarity 2. A base-support Pal that buffs your other workers' Gathering.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="1" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **1 / 10** — very low, cheap to keep fed.

## Partner skill

**Happy Clover** — while assigned at a base, it raises the
[[gathering|Gathering]] Work Suitability level of **all other base Pals by +1**
(does not stack). The bonus is a flat **+1** — it does not scale with skill
level. A strong pick to leave working at a gathering-focused base.

## Combat

[[elements|Grass]]/[[elements|Neutral]] dual type — weak to Fire (Grass) and Dark
(Neutral). Decent melee (MeleeAttack 100) but low Attack/Defense (65). At level
80 it reaches Health 4100–5060, Attack 490–607, Defense 440–557.
