# Katress Ignis <small>#79B</small>

> Due to its upbringing, it no longer cares about whether its food is raw enough.
> This Pal just prefers its food as spicy as possible. Not on good terms with
> [[wixen-noct|Wixen Noct]].

The [[elements|Dark]]/[[elements|Fire]] variant of [[katress|Katress]] (#79) —
Paldeck #79B, size M, rarity 6. A strong base worker whose partner skill boosts
the whole base's Kindling.

## Food

<span class="food-meter" data-value="4" data-max="10"></span>

Consumption **4 / 10** — moderate appetite.

## Partner skill

**Blazing Black Magic** — while at a base, it raises the [[kindling|Kindling]] Work
Suitability level of **all other base Pals by +1** (flat at every skill level,
**does not stack**). Excellent for boosting furnace/forge throughput without a
dedicated high-Kindling Pal.

## Combat

[[elements|Dark]]/[[elements|Fire]] dual type — weak to Dragon and Water. High
offense (Attack 105, Melee 100). At level 80 it reaches Health 4700–5840, Attack
730–919, Defense 590–752.
