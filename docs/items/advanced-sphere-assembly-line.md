# Advanced Sphere Assembly Line

> The end-game sphere line — advanced machinery makes spheres at very high
> speed. Heavy power draw.

The strongest sphere station, above the
[[sphere-assembly-line-ii|Sphere Assembly Line II]]. It draws 1000
electricity/sec for the highest sphere throughput. Drains a little sanity
(SAN −0.15); defense 5.

## Notable crafts

All capture spheres, from the [[pal-sphere|Pal Sphere]] up to the legendary tier.

## Build

Unlocked at **Technology Lv 58**. Build workload: 500000 ([[handiwork|Handiwork]]).
Requires an electricity supply to operate.
