# Tomato Seeds

> Seeds that produce [[tomato|Tomato]]. Necessary for starting a farm. Can be
> purchased from a merchant.

A crop seed: plant it at a plantation to grow [[tomato|Tomato]]. Cheap to buy
from a merchant, or farmed off Grass Pals.

## Obtain

Bought from a merchant, or dropped by several Grass Pals on capture or defeat:
[[dinossom|Dinossom]], [[dinossom-lux|Dinossom Lux]] and [[bristla|Bristla]]
(×1–2, 100%), [[cinnamoth|Cinnamoth]] (×1, 75%), and [[vaelet|Vaelet]] and
[[braloha|Braloha]] (×1, 50%).

## Used in

- [[tomato|Tomato]] — grown at a farm
