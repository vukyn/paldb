# Giant Pal Soul

> The soul left behind by a highly trained Pal — offer it to a Statue of Power to draw out a Pal's hidden abilities.

The top tier of Pal Soul, giving the largest Souls bonus when offered at a
[[statue-of-power|Statue of Power]]. Made at a [[crusher|Crusher]] from **2
[[large-pal-soul|Large Pal Soul]]**.

## Obtain / convert

Crafted at a [[crusher|Crusher]] / [[refrigerated-crusher|Refrigerated Crusher]]
from [[large-pal-soul|Large Pal Soul]] ×2; also dropped by Pals. Can be broken
back down into Large Pal Souls.

## Used to craft

- [[large-pal-soul|Large Pal Soul]] (break down, →2)
