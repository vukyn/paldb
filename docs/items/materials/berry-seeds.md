# Berry Seeds

> Seeds that produce Red Berries — needed to start a farm.

The starter crop seed: sow it at a plantation to grow [[red-berries|Red Berries]],
the basic early-game food. Can also be gathered when picking Red Berries in the
wild.

## Obtain

- Gathered when picking [[red-berries|Red Berries]] in the wild; also buyable
  from a merchant.
- Dropped by Grass Pals on capture or defeat, e.g. [[gumoss|Gumoss]] (×1),
  [[ribbuny|Ribbuny]] (×1) or [[clovee|Clovee]] (×1–2), all 100%.
