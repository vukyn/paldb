# Crude Oil

> A material that can be processed into various products, mainly pumped from oil fields.

A base industrial material that refines into higher-tech products. It is
primarily obtained by installing a [[crude-oil-extractor|Crude Oil Extractor]] on
an oil field, and also drops from some Pals.

## Obtain

- Install a [[crude-oil-extractor|Crude Oil Extractor]] on an oil field to pump it
  passively.
- Dropped by certain Pals on capture or defeat, e.g. [[sootseer|Sootseer]] (×1, 50%).
