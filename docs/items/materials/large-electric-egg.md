# Large Electric Egg

> A Large Electric Egg. A pulsing energy stirs within. Place it in an Egg Incubator to incubate it.

The large, Rare-tier Electric-themed egg. Put it in an [[egg-incubator|Egg Incubator]]
to incubate and hatch the Pal inside. Electric Pals such as [[rayhound|Rayhound]]
hatch from this egg type.

## Obtain

Found in nests near ruins/highlands, or produced by [[breeding]] two Pals whose
result hatches from a Large Electric Egg.
