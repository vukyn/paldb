# Large Pal Soul

> A spirit left behind by a large Pal — offer it to a Statue of Power to draw out a Pal's hidden abilities.

The second-highest tier of Pal Soul. Offered at a [[statue-of-power|Statue of
Power]] to enhance a Pal. At a [[crusher|Crusher]]: **2
[[medium-pal-soul|Medium]] → 1 Large**, and **2 Large → 1
[[giant-pal-soul|Giant Pal Soul]]**.

## Obtain / convert

Crafted at a [[crusher|Crusher]] / [[refrigerated-crusher|Refrigerated Crusher]]
from [[medium-pal-soul|Medium Pal Soul]] ×2 (or break down a
[[giant-pal-soul|Giant Pal Soul]]); also dropped by Pals.

## Used to craft

- [[giant-pal-soul|Giant Pal Soul]] (×2 → 1)
- [[medium-pal-soul|Medium Pal Soul]] (break down, →2)
