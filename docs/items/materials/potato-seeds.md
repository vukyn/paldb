# Potato Seeds

> Seeds that produce [[potato|Potato]]. Necessary for starting a farm.

A crop seed: plant it at a plantation to grow [[potato|Potato]]. Farmed off Grass
Pals or produced at a Ranch.

## Obtain

Dropped by several Pals on capture or defeat: [[robinquill|Robinquill]] and
[[robinquill-terra|Robinquill Terra]] (×1–2, 100%), and [[petallia|Petallia]]
(×1, 100%). Also produced at a [[ranch|Ranch]] by [[vaelet|Vaelet]]'s Purification
of Gaia skill.

## Used in

- [[potato|Potato]] — grown at a farm
