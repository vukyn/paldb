# Large Scorching Egg

> A Large Scorching Egg. Passion burns from within. Place it in an Egg Incubator to incubate it.

The large, Rare-tier Fire-themed egg. Put it in an [[egg-incubator|Egg Incubator]]
to incubate and hatch the Pal inside. Fire Pals such as [[wixen|Wixen]] and [[wixen-noct|Wixen Noct]] hatch from this
egg type.

## Obtain

Found in nests in volcanic/hot biomes, or produced by [[breeding]] two Pals whose
result hatches from a Large Scorching Egg.
