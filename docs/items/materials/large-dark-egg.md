# Large Dark Egg

> A Large Dark Egg. A vicious wail howls within. Place it in an Egg Incubator to incubate it.

The large, Rare-tier Dark-themed egg, a rarer counterpart of the common
[[dark-egg|Dark Egg]]. Put it in an [[egg-incubator|Egg Incubator]] to incubate
and hatch the Pal inside. Dark Pals such as [[katress|Katress]] and [[katress-ignis|Katress Ignis]] hatch
from this egg type.

## Obtain

Found in nests, or produced by [[breeding]] two Pals whose result hatches from a
Large Dark Egg.
