# Large Frozen Egg

> A Large Frozen Egg — an icy pulse pounds within. Place it in an Egg Incubator to
> hatch it.

The large Ice egg, a rarer counterpart of the common [[frozen-egg|Frozen Egg]]. It
hatches big Ice Pals such as [[sweepa|Sweepa]]. Put it in an
[[egg-incubator|Egg Incubator]] to hatch it.

## Obtain

Found in nests in cold biomes, or produced by [[breeding]] two Pals whose result
hatches from a Large Frozen Egg.
