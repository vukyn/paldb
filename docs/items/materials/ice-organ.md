# Ice Organ

> A material harvested from Ice Pals. Feeds cooling gear and ice recipes.

Dropped by Ice-element Pals on capture or defeat. Used in cooling recipes such as
[[cryogenic-coolant|Cryogenic Coolant]] and to craft ice gear like the
[[ice-mine|Ice Mine]].

## Obtain

Dropped by Ice-element Pals on capture or defeat — e.g. [[jolthog-cryst|Jolthog Cryst]]
(×1), [[pengullet|Pengullet]] (×1–2), [[hangyu-cryst|Hangyu Cryst]] (×1),
[[penking|Penking]] (×1–3), [[rayhound-cryst|Rayhound Cryst]] (×1–2),
[[foxcicle|Foxcicle]] (×2–3), [[kingpaca-cryst|Kingpaca Cryst]] (×3–6) or
[[reindrix|Reindrix]] (×2–3), all 100%.

## Used in

- [[cryogenic-coolant|Cryogenic Coolant]] (×1)
- [[ice-mine|Ice Mine]] (×2)
