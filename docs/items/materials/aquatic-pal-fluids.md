# Aquatic Pal Fluids

> Viscous bodily fluids from Water Pals — a handy crafting material.

Dropped by Water-element Pals on capture or defeat. Its high viscosity makes it
useful in binding and cooling recipes like [[cement|Cement]] and
[[cryogenic-coolant|Cryogenic Coolant]].

## Obtain

Dropped by Water-element Pals on capture or defeat — e.g. [[fuack|Fuack]],
[[fuack-ignis|Fuack Ignis]], [[celaray|Celaray]], [[celaray-lux|Celaray Lux]], and [[croajiro|Croajiro]].

## Used in

- [[cement|Cement]] (×1)
- [[cryogenic-coolant|Cryogenic Coolant]] (×1)
