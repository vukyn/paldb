# High Quality Cloth

> A refined textile woven from a large amount of Wool — required for high quality armor.

[[leather|Leather]]. Required to create high quality armor, and used in late-game
structures. Unlocked at Technology Lv 36 and crafted at a
[[high-quality-workbench|High Quality Workbench]].
