# Ingot

> Required to craft metal weapons and armor. Refined from [[ore|Ore]] in a
> furnace.

## Obtain

Refine [[ore|Ore]] ×2 in a [[primitive-furnace|Primitive Furnace]] (or any
higher furnace / the [[ancient-furnace|Ancient Furnace]]) → 1 Ingot.

## Use

Craft metal weapons and armor, and [[gold-coin|Gold Coin]].
