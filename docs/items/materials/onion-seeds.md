# Onion Seeds

> Seeds that produce [[onion|Onion]]. Necessary for starting a farm.

A crop seed: plant it at a plantation to grow [[onion|Onion]]. Farmed off Grass
Pals or produced at a Ranch.

## Obtain

Dropped by several Pals on capture or defeat: [[broncherry|Broncherry]],
[[broncherry-aqua|Broncherry Aqua]] and [[palumba|Palumba]] (×1–2, 100%), and
[[vaelet|Vaelet]] (×1, 50%). Also produced at a [[ranch|Ranch]] by
[[vaelet|Vaelet]]'s Purification of Gaia skill.

## Used in

- [[onion|Onion]] — grown at a farm
