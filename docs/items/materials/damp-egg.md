# Damp Egg

> A Damp Egg — something swims within. Place it in an Egg Incubator to hatch it.

The Water-themed common egg. Put it in an [[egg-incubator|Egg Incubator]] to
incubate and hatch the Pal inside. Found near water or laid via breeding — Water
Pals such as [[pengullet|Pengullet]] and [[pengullet-lux|Pengullet Lux]] hatch
from this egg type.

## Obtain

Found in nests near water, or produced by [[breeding]] two Pals whose result
hatches from a Damp Egg.
