# Flame Organ

> A material harvested from Fire Pals. Feeds furnaces and fire-based recipes.

Dropped by Fire-element Pals (capture or defeat). A staple mid-game material:
it fuels every furnace tier and crafts fire gear such as the [[fire-arrow|Fire Arrow]].

## Obtain

Dropped by Fire-element Pals on capture or defeat — e.g. [[fuack-ignis|Fuack Ignis]] (×1–2, 50%).

## Used to build

- [[primitive-furnace|Primitive Furnace]] (×3)
- [[improved-furnace|Improved Furnace]] (×15)
- [[gigantic-furnace|Gigantic Furnace]] (×200)
