# Huge Frozen Egg

> A Huge Frozen Egg. A frigid spirit stirs within — as if a monster is about to be born. Place it in an Egg Incubator to incubate it.

The huge, Legendary-tier Ice-themed egg, the largest counterpart of the common
[[frozen-egg|Frozen Egg]]. Put it in an [[egg-incubator|Egg Incubator]] to incubate
and hatch the Pal inside. Big Ice Pals such as [[kingpaca-cryst|Kingpaca Cryst]]
hatch from this egg type.

## Obtain

Found in nests in cold biomes, or produced by [[breeding]] two Pals whose result
hatches from a Huge Frozen Egg.
