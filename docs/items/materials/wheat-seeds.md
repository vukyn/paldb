# Wheat Seeds

> Seeds that produce Wheat — needed to start a farm. Also buyable from a
> merchant.

The starter crop seed: sow it at a plantation to grow [[wheat|Wheat]], which
mills into [[flour|Flour]]. Cheap and easy to stock.
