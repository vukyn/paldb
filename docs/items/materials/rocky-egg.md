# Rocky Egg

> A Rocky Egg — something calm stirs within. Place it in an Egg Incubator to hatch it.

The Ground-themed common egg. Put it in an [[egg-incubator|Egg Incubator]] to
incubate and hatch the Pal inside. Ground-leaning Pals such as
[[pupperai|Pupperai]] hatch from this egg type.

## Obtain

Found in nests in the world, or produced by [[breeding]] two Pals whose result
hatches from a Rocky Egg.
