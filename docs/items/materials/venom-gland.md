# Venom Gland

> Material obtainable from poisonous Pals — used to craft Poison Arrow and more.

A crafting material harvested from poison-type Pals. Its main use is making
[[poison-arrow|Poison Arrow]] and other poison-based gear.

## Obtain

Dropped by poison-type / Dark Pals on capture or defeat, e.g.
[[croajiro-noct|Croajiro Noct]] (×2–4), [[depresso|Depresso]] (×2–3) or
[[daedream|Daedream]] (×1), all 100%.
Also produced by [[depresso|Depresso]] on a [[ranch|Ranch]] via Caffeine Inoculation.

## Used in

- [[poison-arrow|Poison Arrow]]
