# Huge Verdant Egg

> A Huge Verdant Egg. A verdant spirit grows within — as if a monster is about to
> be born. Place it in an Egg Incubator to incubate it.

The largest, legendary Grass egg — the oversized counterpart of the common
[[verdant-egg|Verdant Egg]]. It hatches big / high-rarity Grass Pals such as
[[vaelet|Vaelet]], [[petallia|Petallia]], [[petallia-ignis|Petallia Ignis]] and
[[elizabee|Elizabee]]. Put it in an [[egg-incubator|Egg Incubator]] to hatch it.

## Obtain

Found in nests in the world, or produced by [[breeding]] two Pals whose result
hatches from a Huge Verdant Egg.
