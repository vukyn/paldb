# Large Damp Egg

> A Large Damp Egg. Purity swirls within. Place it in an Egg Incubator to incubate it.

The large, Rare-tier Water-themed egg. Put it in an [[egg-incubator|Egg Incubator]]
to incubate and hatch the Pal inside. Heavier (weight 25) than the common
[[damp-egg|Damp Egg]]. Water Pals such as [[penking|Penking]] hatch from this egg type.

## Obtain

Found in nests near water, or produced by [[breeding]] two Pals whose result
hatches from a Large Damp Egg.
