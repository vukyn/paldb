# Leather

> Leather taken from a Pal — a defensive crafting material used in armor and gear.

A common early-game material obtained from certain Pals. It serves as a defensive
material, feeding into armor and other protective crafts.

## Obtain

Dropped by certain Pals on capture or defeat, including [[fuack|Fuack]],
[[fuack-ignis|Fuack Ignis]], [[vixy|Vixy]], [[herbil|Herbil]], [[ribbuny|Ribbuny]], [[ribbuny-botan|Ribbuny Botan]], [[foxcicle|Foxcicle]], [[melpaca|Melpaca]], [[katress|Katress]], and
[[reindrix|Reindrix]] (×1–2, 100%).
