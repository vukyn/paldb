# Carrot Seeds

> Seeds that produce [[carrot|Carrot]]. Necessary for starting a farm.

An early-game crop seed. Plant it at a Berry Plantation (or equivalent farm) to
grow [[carrot|Carrot]] — a renewable food source for your base Pals.

## Obtain

Dropped by several Grass and forest Pals on capture or defeat: [[ribbuny-botan|Ribbuny Botan]],
[[dinossom-lux|Dinossom Lux]], [[mossanda|Mossanda]] and [[palumba|Palumba]]
(×1–2, 100%), and [[wumpo-botan|Wumpo Botan]] and [[prunelia|Prunelia]] (×1, 50%).

## Used in

- [[carrot-plantation|Carrot Plantation]] — planted here to grow [[carrot|Carrot]] (also ×3 to build it)
