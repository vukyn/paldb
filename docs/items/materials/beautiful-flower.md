# Beautiful Flower

> A plant that blooms colorful flowers. Can be used to make [[suspicious-juice|Suspicious Juice]]
> and [[strange-juice|Strange Juice]].

A decorative flower gathered in the wild. Beyond looking nice it feeds a couple
of juice recipes and slightly restores sanity when consumed (SAN +5).

## Obtain

Gathered in the wild; dropped by [[petallia|Petallia]] and
[[petallia-ignis|Petallia Ignis]] (×2–3, 100%) and as a rare drop from
[[ribbuny|Ribbuny]] (×1, 5%).

## Used in

- [[suspicious-juice|Suspicious Juice]]
- [[strange-juice|Strange Juice]]

## Used to build

- [[flower-bed|Flower Bed]] (×10)
