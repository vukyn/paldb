# Huge Common Egg

> A Huge Common Egg. A gentle spirit throbs within — as if a monster is about to be born. Place it in an Egg Incubator to incubate it.

The huge, Legendary-tier Neutral-themed egg, the largest counterpart of the common
[[common-egg|Common Egg]]. Put it in an [[egg-incubator|Egg Incubator]] to incubate
and hatch the Pal inside. Big Neutral Pals such as [[kingpaca|Kingpaca]] hatch from
this egg type.

## Obtain

Found in nests, or produced by [[breeding]] two Pals whose result hatches from a
Huge Common Egg.
