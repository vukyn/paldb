# Ore

> Becomes an [[ingot|Ingot]] when refined in a furnace. Found in caves and other
> places.

## Obtain

Mined from ore nodes in caves and around the map ([[mining|Mining]]).

## Use

Refine ×2 in a furnace → 1 [[ingot|Ingot]]. Also a component of
[[refined-ingot|Refined Ingot]] and [[pal-metal-ingot|Pal Metal Ingot]].
