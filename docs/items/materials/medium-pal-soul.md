# Medium Pal Soul

> A spirit left behind by a Pal — offer it to a Statue of Power to draw out a Pal's hidden abilities.

The mid tier of Pal Soul. Offered at a [[statue-of-power|Statue of Power]] to
enhance a Pal. At a [[crusher|Crusher]] souls convert both ways: **2
[[small-pal-soul|Small]] → 1 Medium**, and **2 Medium → 1
[[large-pal-soul|Large Pal Soul]]**.

## Obtain / convert

- Craft at a [[crusher|Crusher]] / [[refrigerated-crusher|Refrigerated Crusher]]
  from [[small-pal-soul|Small Pal Soul]] ×2 (or break down a
  [[large-pal-soul|Large Pal Soul]]).
- Dropped by certain Pals on capture or defeat, e.g. [[bushi-noct|Bushi Noct]]
  (×1–2, 40%) and [[sootseer|Sootseer]] (×1–2, 100%).

## Used to craft

- [[large-pal-soul|Large Pal Soul]] (×2 → 1)
- [[small-pal-soul|Small Pal Soul]] (break down, →2)
