# Paldium Fragment

> Crystallized energy from the World Tree. Used to create supernatural items and
> structures.

## Obtain

- Crafted from [[stone|Stone]] ×5.
- Gathered from blue Paldium nodes around the map.
- Extracted from [[meteorite-fragment|Meteorite Fragment]] in a [[crusher|Crusher]].

## Use

Core material for capture spheres — every [[pal-sphere|Pal Sphere]],
[[mega-sphere|Mega Sphere]], [[giga-sphere|Giga Sphere]],
[[hyper-sphere|Hyper Sphere]], [[ultra-sphere|Ultra Sphere]] and [[legendary-sphere|Legendary Sphere]] needs
it — plus the [[pal-metal-ingot|Pal Metal Ingot]] alloy, the Palbox, and other
"supernatural" builds.
