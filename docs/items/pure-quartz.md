# Pure Quartz

> Pure Quartz, often found in snowy fields and similar places. Required to make
> Circuit Boards.

## Obtain

- Mined in snowy fields and similar cold areas.
- Dropped by [[pierdon-cryst|Pierdon Cryst]] (×4–5, 100%) — including its Lv.70
  variant.

## Use

Craft [[circuit-board|Circuit Board]] and the [[pal-metal-ingot|Pal Metal Ingot]].
