# Sphere Workbench

> The starter station dedicated to making capture spheres. Small and slow, but
> the first place you can mass-produce your own [[pal-sphere|Pal Spheres]].

The entry sphere station — a bench-tier [[handiwork|Handiwork]] station devoted
to spheres, so you stop hand-crafting them one at a time. The small workspace
keeps output slow. Drains a little sanity (SAN −0.15); defense 2.

## Notable crafts

All capture spheres, from the [[pal-sphere|Pal Sphere]] upward.

## Build

Unlocked at **Technology Lv 14**. Build workload: 5000 ([[handiwork|Handiwork]]).
