# Gigantic Furnace

> A huge smelter — refines [[hexolite|Hexolite]]. Its size lets several Pals
> smelt together. Needs a Fire Pal for the flame.

The end-game furnace, the only one that makes [[hexolite|Hexolite]]. Its large
size lets multiple Pals collaborate on one job, and it draws 1500
electricity/sec. Needs the [[kindling|Kindling]] suitability. Low sanity drain
(SAN −0.08); defense 5.

## Refines

- [[hexolite|Hexolite]].

## Build

Unlocked at **Technology Lv 58**. Build workload: 600000 ([[handiwork|Handiwork]]).
Requires an electricity supply to operate.
