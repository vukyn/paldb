# Ice Mine

> A floor trap that freezes any Pal stepping on it. A frozen Pal is far easier
> to catch.

A capture aid: place it where a wild Pal will walk, and the freeze it inflicts
raises your sphere's catch rate on that Pal. A Defenses-type placeable, unlocked
at Technology Lv 38.

## Build

Unlocked at **Technology Lv 38**.
