# Meteorite Fragment

> A fragment of rock from outer space. Holds large amounts of Paldium — extract
> it by placing the fragment in a Crusher.

## Obtain

Gathered from meteor-strike sites around the map, and dropped by certain raid
bosses:

| Pal | Qty | Chance |
|-----|:---:|:------:|
| [Xenolord](../pals/xenolord.md) | ×7–15 | 100% |
| [Xenovader](../pals/xenovader.md) | ×3–10 | 100% |
| [Xenogard](../pals/xenogard.md) | ×5–12 | 100% |

## Use

Place in a [[crusher|Crusher]] to extract [[paldium-fragment|Paldium Fragment]].
