# Production Assembly Line II

> The top-tier assembly line — tight management pushes production speed far past
> the first line. Draws heavy power.

The best [[handiwork|Handiwork]] station in the game, an upgrade over the
[[production-assembly-line|Production Assembly Line]]. It doubles the power draw
(400 electricity/sec) for the highest crafting throughput. Drains more sanity
(SAN −0.2); defense 4.

## Notable crafts

Makes [[gunpowder|Gunpowder]] and the highest-tier items at full speed.

## Build

Unlocked at **Technology Lv 42**. Build workload: 50000 ([[handiwork|Handiwork]]).
Requires an electricity supply to operate.
