# Advanced Technical Manual

> A book that contains secrets of amazing technology. Use it to obtain a few Technology Points.

A consumable that grants **+1 Technology Point** on use — a way to unlock tech
without leveling up. Dropped rarely by knowledge-themed Dark/Fire Pals.

## Use

Consume to gain **1 Technology Point**, spendable in the technology tree.

## Obtain

A **1% drop** from wise/witch-themed Pals — [[katress|Katress]], [[wixen|Wixen]],
[[hoocrates|Hoocrates]], [[wixen-noct|Wixen Noct]] and [[katress-ignis|Katress Ignis]]
(and their alpha/boss variants), ×1 each.
