# Honey

> Honey harvested from [[beegarde|Beegarde]]. Sweet and tasty.

A sweet ingredient used in baking (e.g. Cake). Slightly restores sanity when
eaten (SAN +3).

## Obtain

Dropped by several bee/insect Pals on capture or defeat: [[elizabee|Elizabee]]
(×5, 100%), [[warsect|Warsect]] and [[warsect-terra|Warsect Terra]] (×3–4, 100%),
[[beegarde|Beegarde]] (×1–2, 100%) and [[cinnamoth|Cinnamoth]] (×1, 100%). Also
produced by [[beegarde|Beegarde]] at a [[ranch|Ranch]].
