# Carrot

> A vibrant, nutrient-rich root vegetable. Not suitable for raw consumption.

A common crop grown from [[carrot-seeds|Carrot Seeds]] at a
[[carrot-plantation|Carrot Plantation]]. It is an ingredient — cook it into
dishes rather than eating it raw.

## Obtain

Grown at a [[carrot-plantation|Carrot Plantation]] from
[[carrot-seeds|Carrot Seeds]]; also dropped by [[ribbuny-botan|Ribbuny Botan]] on
capture or defeat (×1, 100%).
