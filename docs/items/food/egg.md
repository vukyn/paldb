> A basic food ingredient.

## Obtain

- Guaranteed drop when you capture or defeat [[chikipi|Chikipi]] (×1).
- Laid by [[chikipi|Chikipi]] on a [[ranch|Ranch]] via its **Egg Layer** partner skill (amount
  scales with skill level).
