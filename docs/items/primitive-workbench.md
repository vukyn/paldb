# Primitive Workbench

> The first crafting bench — makes simple early items by hand. Needs a Pal with
> the [[handiwork|Handiwork]] suitability, or you can craft at it yourself.

The starter [[handiwork|Handiwork]] station, unlocked from the very first
Technology point. A Pal assigned here (or the player) turns recipes into items;
drains a little sanity (SAN −0.11). Defense 2.

## Notable crafts

Simple early items — including [[nail|Nail]] and the basic [[pal-sphere|Pal Sphere]].

## Build

Unlocked at **Technology Lv 1**. Build workload: 500 ([[handiwork|Handiwork]]).
