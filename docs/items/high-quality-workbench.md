# High Quality Workbench

> A compact assembly line for items and gear. The small workspace caps its
> speed, but it unlocks recipes the primitive bench can't make.

The mid-game [[handiwork|Handiwork]] station — a step up from the
[[primitive-workbench|Primitive Workbench]] that opens more advanced crafts. The
cramped bench limits throughput. Drains a little sanity (SAN −0.11); defense 2.

## Notable crafts

Makes [[gunpowder|Gunpowder]], [[cement|Cement]], and other early materials.

## Build

Unlocked at **Technology Lv 11**. Build workload: 3000 ([[handiwork|Handiwork]]).
